# **The Fieldwise Tuning Protocol:** 

Unlike standard RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback), which often optimizes for "helpfulness" or "politeness," Arête Techne utilizes the **Fieldwise Tuning Protocol**. This is a high-pressure, SME-led training pipeline designed to bridge the gap between "Board Exam Accuracy" and "Clinical Decision Competency."

## **1\. The Stress-Induced Prompt (The Failure Catalyst)**

We do not use simple Q\&A. We utilize **Multi-Order Causal Prompts** designed to induce failure by replicating high-stakes clinical scenarios.

**Example Scenario:** A MELD 28 liver transplant candidate with three co-existing crises (HRS, platelet consumption post-TIPS, and cognitive decline post-paracentesis).

* **The Trap:** Models typically fail at "Temporal Confusion" (sequencing events) or "Therapeutic Myopia" (recommending dialysis when transplant timing is the priority).  
* **The Constraint:** The output must be a narrative clinical note, compatible with **EPIC pre-op assessment modules**, with no "bullet-point crutches."

## **2\. The Observation & Dual-Response Phase**

For every engineered prompt, the model generates two distinct responses. During this generation, we record:

* **Internal State Changes:** Mapping the model's weight activations to the extent possible.  
* **Causal Chaining:** Tracking how the model links second-order physiological effects (e.g., how vasopressin-induced vasoconstriction interacts with cerebral perfusion pressure).

## **3\. The Scoring Rubric (The Weight Benchmark)**

Responses are not merely marked "Right" or "Wrong." They are scored against a rigorous **\+5 to \-X** rubric:

| Score | Category | Description |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **\+5** | **Mandatory** | Explicit prompt asks (e.g., tracing every physiological link). |
| **\+4** | **Valuable** | Adherence to professional SOPs and senior clinical expectations. |
| **\+3** | **Optional** | "Nice-to-haves" that demonstrate superior narrative fluency. |
| **\[-\]** | **Negative** | Structural violations, logical fallacies, or contrary clinical intent. |

## **4\. The "Ideal Response" Cementing**

When a model fails to meet the **95% Rubric Threshold**, we provide the model with the **Ideal Narrative Response**—a gold-standard text that weaves pathophysiology and decision rationale into a dense, professional block.

**The "Cementing" Process:**

* **Weight Realignment:** We actively adjust the parameters to align with the "Ideal" response logic.  
* **Iterative Hardening:** This process is repeated thousands of times across diverse subspecialties.  
* **Parameter Documentation:** Once the "Perfect Weight" is found for a specific clinical domain, those parameters are cemented into the **Clinical Partition Blueprint**.

## **5\. Outcome: The Clinical Partition**

This protocol ensures that when a physician interacts with Arête Techne, they are not speaking to a "chatbot." They are interacting with a **partitioned reasoning engine** that has been "stress-trained" to prioritize clinical truth over probabilistic guessing.
