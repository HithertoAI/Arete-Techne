## **🧭 The Mission: A Director’s Conscience**

Current AI deployment in medicine suffers from a "Trust Deficit." While national defense and intelligence sectors utilize secure, partitioned AI, over **400,000 physicians** are forced to rely on consumer-grade General Knowledge Models (GKMs) for life-critical decisions. Using a generalist prediction engine for clinical diagnosis is a violation of the Hippocratic Oath.

**Arête Techne** is a domain-specific, logically isolated infrastructure designed to treat medical data with the rigor of a defense system. We move beyond "Policy Promises" to **Architectural Enforcement** by ensuring that patient data never persists beyond the moment of calculation.

---

## **🏗️ Core Architecture: The Bauta Valve**

The heart of Arête Techne is the **Bauta Valve Protocol**—a stateless-by-design, five-chamber architecture that mandates a total memory-wipe between data batches. By ensuring **Zero-Persistence**, we make the retention or leakage of patient PII (Personally Identifiable Information) mathematically impossible.

### **The 5-Chamber Pipeline**

1. **Chamber A (Receipt):** Ingests raw clinical telemetry and physician instructions into a volatile, temporary buffer.  
2. **Chamber B (Symbolization):** Translates medical jargon and patient data into anonymized symbols. Identity is stripped at the logic gate.  
3. **Chamber C (Evaluation):** Processes the symbols against the **Clinical Partition** (The *Techne*).  
4. **Chamber D (The Auditor):** A recursive verification layer that checks the output against the original physician intent to prevent hallucinations.  
5. **Chamber E (Interface):** Delivers the validated result to the physician and triggers a mandatory **Hardware-Level Wipe** of all previous chambers.
